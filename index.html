Landing template for Comporta Ethics

This project is an informative website that I am building for a company called Comporta Ethics and the goal of this company is to basically help and guide potential future buyers for a house, plot of land, or any other type of construction that can legally be built in the area, therefore, the CEO of Comporta Ethics will pretty much do all the searching and project building for the client.

About me

I got the idea of making this project my portfolio from the moment that I knew I would have a project to make to finalize my year. I think that the idea was already in my head by the end of october, but i was sure about it by the end of the second trimester.

But why this project?
I chose this project for two reasons:

First: the company Comporta Ethics is owned by my father, therefore, I always told him that I would be the developper building his website.

Secondly: next year I want to choose the option 'Full Stack', therefore, I thought that it would be intelligent to make a website for my first year exam (already doing more or less what i will be doing next year).
